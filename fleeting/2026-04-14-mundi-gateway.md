# Mundi Gateway

> Raw information capture. Source: `confluence`.

## Content

Mundi Gateway is referenced as the Pagar.me gateway system. In the new payments modeling, retrocompatibility entities (Customer, Merchant Gateway) are created in Mundi Gateway during the transition period for link/ecommerce POI types. Once fully migrated to [[payments-lifecycle]], Gateway creation will no longer be necessary.
